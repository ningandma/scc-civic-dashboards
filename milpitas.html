Commissioner Berryhill champions dementia awareness. Drum circles, music events at Sunnyvale Senior Center. "Paddles Against Dementia" event. Four-part caretaker series.

Ongoing since 2017 Age-Friendly Task Force

Annual

Commission participates in Silicon Valley Walk. First joined Sept 2023. Sept 28, 2024 event brought new/returning attendees. Close to matching prior year donations.

Annual Work Plan

FY Aligned

12-month work plan aligned to fiscal year. Presented to City Council each May (Older Americans Month). Five target areas of focus for senior community needs.

Council presentation 5 advisory areas

Sock Hop & Senior Events

Annual

Themed dances (Sock Hop), birthday celebrations, multi-cultural events. Oct 18, 2024 Sock Hop 4-6pm. Commission volunteers for setup and promotion.

Commissioner volunteers Community outreach

Technology for Seniors

Work Plan Area

Digital literacy program. Part of ongoing work plan. Presentations and hands-on support for senior center members.

Senior Nutrition Program

Daily Service

Congregate meals at Barbara Lee Senior Center. ~79 daily avg (March 2019). Outreach Lunch Riders transportation (52-68 one-way trips/month).
